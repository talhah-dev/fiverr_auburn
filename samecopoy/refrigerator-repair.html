Common Refrigerator Problems Fridge Repair Techs Can Fix

Refrigerator Making Loud or Unusual Noises

Strange noises coming from your refrigerator are often a sign of underlying mechanical issues. A worn-out evaporator fan motor or a faulty condenser fan can cause loud humming, buzzing, or clicking sounds. Ignoring these noises could lead to motor failure or costly repairs. Let an Auburn-based appliance expert diagnose the issue and get your fridge running quietly again.

Water Leaking from the Refrigerator

A leaking fridge can cause water damage and mold growth. Common culprits include a clogged defrost drain, a malfunctioning water inlet valve, or a damaged door seal. A professional repair technician will quickly locate the source of the leak and provide an effective solution to prevent further problems.

Refrigerator Won’t Turn On

If your refrigerator isn’t powering up, first check that it’s properly plugged in and that the circuit breaker hasn’t tripped. If that’s not the issue, it could be a faulty compressor relay, a defective temperature control board, or an electrical problem. A skilled repair expert in Auburn can diagnose the issue and restore your refrigerator to full functionality.

Ice Maker Not Producing Ice

If your ice maker has stopped working, the problem could be a clogged water filter, a faulty water inlet valve, or incorrect freezer temperatures. Ice makers also fail due to water line blockages or a malfunctioning thermostat. Any refrigerator repair technician in Auburn that is sent out to you is trained to repair all major ice maker models, ensuring you always have fresh ice when you need it.

Refrigerator Not Cooling Properly

Food spoiling too quickly or freezing unexpectedly? Inconsistent temperatures could be caused by a damaged air inlet damper, a faulty thermistor, or a failing temperature control board. A full system diagnostic by a local repair specialist will identify the root cause and restore proper cooling.

Water Dispenser Not Working

A broken water dispenser is often the result of a clogged filter, a malfunctioning water inlet valve, or issues with the dispenser actuator. Whether you own a Whirlpool, Samsung, GE, or Sub-Zero refrigerator, let Auburn Appliance Repair Pros connect you with an experienced technician that can fix the problem efficiently.

Door Seal & Light Issues

A worn-out door gasket can cause cold air to escape, leading to temperature fluctuations and higher energy costs. Replacing the door seal ensures that your refrigerator operates efficiently. While at it, a technician can also replace any burned-out interior lights for better visibility.
