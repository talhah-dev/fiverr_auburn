Common Electric Appliances Serviced

Electric appliances can be found throughout the home, especially in kitchens and laundry rooms. Here’s a breakdown of the most common types repaired in Auburn:

Electric Ranges & Cooktops

Known for their sleek design and even heating, electric stoves and cooktops are a popular choice. But they can develop issues such as:

Burners not heating or heating unevenly

Glass cooktop cracks or electrical arcs

Faulty control knobs or electronic displays

Tripped breakers or sparking outlets

Electric Ovens

Electric ovens provide consistent baking results and often come with advanced features like convection fans and self-cleaning cycles. Common problems include:

Oven won’t heat or takes too long to preheat

Inaccurate temperature readings

Heating elements not glowing red or uneven cooking

Self-clean function not working or door stuck

Electric Dryers

Electric dryers are efficient, easy to install, and a go-to option for many homes. But like any appliance, they can run into trouble. Issues frequently repaired include:

Drum won’t spin or turns slowly

Dryer runs but produces no heat

Burning smells, excessive lint buildup, or tripped thermostats

Dryer doesn’t shut off or timer malfunctions

Other Electric Appliances

From electric wall ovens to compact countertop appliances, troubleshoot nearly any issue you’re experiencing. If it’s powered by electricity and giving you trouble, there’s a good chance it can repaired.

Signs Your Electric Appliance May Need Service

Not sure if your appliance is due for repair? Here are some common symptoms to watch for:

Breaker frequently trips when appliance is in use

Appliance doesn’t respond or shuts off mid-cycle

Visible sparks or burning odor near power connection

Unusual noises or performance issues

Electrical components can wear out over time. When that happens, it’s important to get a qualified technician involved to prevent further damage—or potential safety hazards.
